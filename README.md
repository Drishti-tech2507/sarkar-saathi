Sarkar Saathi

Every Benefit. One Simple Platform.

Sarkar Saathi is an AI-powered welfare discovery platform designed to bridge the gap between citizens and government welfare schemes. The platform helps users discover relevant subsidies, scholarships, healthcare benefits, pension schemes, farmer welfare programs, and other government initiatives through a simple, multilingual, and accessible interface.

Built with the vision of making government benefits easier to discover, understand, and access, Sarkar Saathi leverages Artificial Intelligence to provide personalized recommendations based on a user’s profile and needs.

⸻

🚨 Problem Statement

Millions of eligible citizens fail to benefit from government welfare programs because information is scattered across multiple portals, often available only in complex formats and limited languages. Rural populations, women, students, senior citizens, and first-time internet users frequently struggle to identify schemes that match their needs.

Key challenges include:

* Lack of awareness about available schemes
* Language barriers
* Complex eligibility criteria
* Poor accessibility for non-technical users
* Fragmented government information systems

⸻

💡 Our Solution

Sarkar Saathi provides a centralized AI-powered platform where users can:

* Discover government schemes tailored to their profile
* Interact through a multilingual AI assistant
* Search benefits using text or voice
* Understand eligibility requirements in simple language
* Receive personalized welfare recommendations

The platform simplifies access to public welfare information and promotes digital inclusion for all citizens.

⸻

✨ Key Features

🤖 AI Saathi Assistant

An intelligent chatbot that answers questions about government schemes and provides personalized recommendations.

🌐 Multilingual Support

Supports multiple languages to ensure accessibility for users across India.

🎤 Voice-Based Interaction

Allows users to interact using voice commands, making the platform easier to use for people with limited literacy.

🎯 Personalized Recommendations

Suggests relevant schemes based on user details and eligibility criteria.

📚 Scheme Discovery Portal

Browse scholarships, healthcare programs, pension schemes, housing benefits, farmer welfare initiatives, and more.

🔍 Smart Search

Quickly find schemes using keywords, categories, or benefit types.

📊 Benefit Matching System

Displays suitable welfare opportunities and eligibility insights.
